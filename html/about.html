Our Vision & Mission

To use Internet technology and the resources it can generate to:

  1. Increase public awareness of the availability of high-quality lost pets
  2. Increase the overall effectiveness of pet rescue programs across Olongapo to the extent that the euthanasia of adoptable pets is eliminated
  3. Elevate the status of pets to that of family member

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is a world where humans and animals coexist in sustainable ecosystems. Our mission is to build awareness and implement effective systems such that habitats and animals are protected.
